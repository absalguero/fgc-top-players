**MenaRD** has officially become a co-owner of Bandits Gaming, confirming his transition from franchise player to organizational leader.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Leadership Expansion:** The legendary competitor is stepping up to help guide the future of Bandits Gaming.
* **Unprecedented Accolades:** MenaRD brings his experience as a two-time Capcom Cup World Champion, three-time EVO Champion, and 2023 SFL World Champion to the executive level.
* **Long-Term Vision:** He aims to build a sustainable esports legacy and develop the next generation of FGC champions.

---

## A Historic Step for Bandits Gaming

The announcement marks a significant milestone for both the competitor and the team. MenaRD has been a central figure in the rise of Bandits Gaming since joining forces with them in 2019. Over the years, his journey evolved from star player into roles like head coach and general manager. This leadership experience helped shape the identity and competitive success of the organization on the global stage. Now, he takes on an ownership stake to push the brand even further into the international spotlight.

The organization views this transition as a core part of its long-term strategy. The overarching goal is to cultivate elite talent while maintaining a strong competitive presence in esports. MenaRD also hinted on social media that fans should expect more roster or organizational moves in the near future. This structural shift highlights a growing trend of top fighting game competitors securing ownership roles to stabilize the community. 

> **"Everything I have accomplished in this game has been connected to the people around me, my teammates, the players I've coached, and the community that raised me as a competitor. Becoming an owner of Bandits isn't just a title change. It's a commitment. I want to build something that outlasts my playing career an organization that finds the next generation of champions and gives them everything they need to reach their highest potential."**
> — *MenaRD*

---

## Unmatched Competitive Pedigree

MenaRD steps into this executive role with a resume that few players in fighting game history can match. He is widely celebrated as the first two-time Capcom Cup World Champion. He claimed his first title in 2017 and secured his second victory in early 2023. His dominance has only accelerated in the Street Fighter 6 era. He is currently a three-time EVO Champion. This incredible feat includes back-to-back victories at EVO Japan in 2024 and 2025 alongside a massive win at EVO Vegas in 2025. 

Furthermore, his success is deeply tied to the Bandits Gaming brand. MenaRD led the team to victory at the 2023 Street Fighter League World Championship. This historic win cemented his ability to build and lead a championship roster. His shift to co-owner means he will now have even more direct influence over the competitive direction of the team. He wants to ensure that upcoming players receive the necessary resources to reach their absolute peak.

This new chapter for MenaRD and Bandits Gaming signals a bright future for the competitive scene in the Dominican Republic and beyond. By moving into a position of ownership, MenaRD is ensuring that his wealth of knowledge and competitive spirit will continue to influence the FGC long after his playing days conclude. Fans eagerly await the next major announcements from the Bandits camp as they prepare to build the next dynasty of Street Fighter champions.
