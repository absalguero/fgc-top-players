Capcom stated on its official [CapcomFighters X account](https://x.com/CapcomFighters/status/1982422369953358253) that it is reviewing pricing for the **Capcom Cup 12 Finals** and the **Street Fighter League World Championship**. The post says the company will share updated information later in October, including the **price of the live viewing ticket** and added context for fans.

## Summary of the update

Capcom confirms that pricing for live viewing is under review. When the review is complete, the company plans to publish the ticket price and the reasoning behind the decision on its official channels. [CapcomFighters on X](https://x.com/CapcomFighters/status/1982422369953358253)

## What this means for viewers

Fans waiting to purchase a live viewing ticket for **Capcom Cup 12** or the **SFL World Championship** should expect an official pricing update before month end. Until the review is finished, prior figures may change.

## Background

This pricing review follows the earlier decision to place the Capcom Cup 12 Finals behind a pay per view model. For details on that move and community reaction, see our earlier report: [Capcom Cup 12 Finals Move to Pay Per View, Sparking FGC Debate](/news/capcom-cup-12-finals-move-to-pay-per-view-sparking-fgc-debate/).
