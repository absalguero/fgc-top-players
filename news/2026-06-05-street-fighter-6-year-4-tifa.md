Capcom is abandoning legacy veterans for an uncharted Street Fighter 6 Year 4 roster headlined by Final Fantasy VII's Tifa Lockhart. The upcoming DLC cycle leaves familiar franchise faces behind in favor of untested blood. Two completely original characters and a prominent World Tour rival will join the Square Enix guest star to shift the competitive landscape.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Tifa Lockhart joins the cast in early 2027 as the first non-fighting game guest character.**
* **The entire season bypasses legacy characters for brand-new challengers and World Tour NPC Bosch.**
* **Yasmine kicks off the content cycle on August 3, 2026, using a fast-paced karambit fighting style.**

</div>

## A Season of Uncharted Matchups

The Year 4 content drops begin on August 3 with Yasmine. This Filipina high schooler wields a crescent-shaped karambit knife. Her toolkit focuses heavily on high-speed movement and close-quarters rushdown pressure. Arjun follows in the autumn to introduce a "Bare-knuckle Yoga" fighting style. He steps onto the grid as a former police officer on the run. The developers indicate his mechanics will rely on an explosive lung capacity system.

Tifa Lockhart enters the battle in early 2027. Following SNK crossovers Terry and Mai, the Avalanche resistance fighter becomes the first guest from an outside RPG universe. She brings her Zangan-ryu martial arts into the 2D plane as a dedicated close-range brawler. Capcom rebuilt her combat animations specifically for the Street Fighter engine while adapting Final Fantasy Materia into a unique gameplay mechanic. Bosch closes out the season in spring 2027. The World Tour protagonist transitions into the playable cast after studying under Luke. His moveset blends modern striking with ancient Nayshall techniques.

Players can expect a steady rollout of these four challengers over the next twelve months. The Character and Ultimate passes are currently available for purchase across all platforms.

## A Shift in Development Strategy

This roster configuration marks a sharp pivot in how Capcom builds its competitive seasons. Previous updates relied heavily on returning icons to maintain community engagement. By anchoring Year 4 with entirely fresh faces and an iconic RPG crossover, the studio forces competitive players to adapt to completely blank slates. Professional players must solve entirely new matchups without years of historic frame data or foundational legacy knowledge to guide them.
