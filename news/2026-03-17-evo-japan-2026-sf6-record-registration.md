**Street Fighter 6** has officially broken the all-time registration record, confirming its status as the most popular title in fighting game history. The upcoming event has reached its maximum capacity of 7,158 players on the start.gg platform.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Street Fighter 6 at EVO Japan 2026 is now the largest fighting game tournament of all time.**
* **The event surpassed the previous record of 7,083 set by Evo Vegas 2023.**
* **High demand has forced organizers to extend registration periods for other titles after the SF6 cap was met.**

---

## Street Fighter 6 Sets New World Record in Japan

The Fighting Game Community has reached a historic milestone as Street Fighter 6 at EVO Japan 2026 officially hit 7,158 entrants. This massive figure secures the tournament's place as the biggest major in the history of the genre. By reaching its player cap, the event has eclipsed the previous gold standard set during the launch year of the game at Evo Vegas 2023.

The explosive growth of the Japanese scene has been a primary driver for these numbers. While previous years saw steady growth, the 2026 iteration of EVO Japan has demonstrated an unprecedented level of player retention and competitive interest. This surge in registration has even led organizers to adjust schedules and registration windows for other featured titles to accommodate the massive scale of the venue.
