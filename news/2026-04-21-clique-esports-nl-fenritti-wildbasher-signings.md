## Expanding the Clique Roster for Club Points

To compete for the lucrative overall team prize pool, organizations are aggressively recruiting players in games where they currently lack representation. Clique Management, operating as an international esports agency, is following this exact blueprint to maximize its tournament footprint. By bringing NL, Fenritti, and WildBasher under one banner, the organization transforms into a serious contender for the EWC Club Championship.

NL is a dedicated Street Fighter 6 specialist. His calculated playstyle guarantees a steady stream of tournament points and ensures Clique has a strong chance at deep bracket runs. Fenritti anchors the roster in a completely different ecosystem as a top-tier threat in Fatal Fury: City of the Wolves. WildBasher similarly fills a specific gap for the brand. Sponsors are realizing that having a presence in every featured game is a necessity for a top payout. Acquiring competitors for unrepresented titles is the most direct path to the top of the leaderboard.

---

## Clique Management's Strategic Vision

Operating primarily as an elite talent management agency, Clique is uniquely positioned to identify and acquire high-value players in the fighting game community. The organization has built a reputation on handling logistics, branding, and career growth for top-tier professionals. By actively signing these veteran competitors, Clique is evolving from a support structure into a dominant competitive brand.

The race for the club championship demands strategic foresight and diverse game coverage. Securing NL, Fenritti, and WildBasher demonstrates Clique understands the exact structural demands required to contend for the overarching EWC team prize. This roster expansion ensures they will not be left behind as the brackets unfold.
