Capcom dropped the official Street Fighter 6 character guide for Yasmine, breaking down her aerial mix-up potential, boomerang setplay, and resource-driven Bayani Mode install.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Bayani Mode Buffs:** Yasmine's core install alters block advantage and expands combo paths, making resource management central to her strategy.
* **Setplay and Mobility:** The Pangil sa Likuran boomerang projectile and Mukha ng Langit leaps grant her distinct space control and ambiguous mix-ups.
* **Invincible Reversals:** OD Lipad ng Agila provides startup invincibility to clear defensive pressure during high-level play.

</div>

## Aerial Mobility and Boomerang Traps Define Yasmine's Kit

Capcom unveiled the complete breakdown for Yasmine in an extensive character overview video, offering tournament players a direct look at her frame dynamics and neutral tools. Her archetype centers around high-speed movement and delayed projectile setups designed to disrupt opponent positioning.

Her core neutral tool, Pangil sa Likuran, launches a boomerang knife that arcs behind the opponent before returning. This multi-hit trajectory forces difficult blocking situations and creates space-lock conditions during grounded approach attempts. Players can layer this setplay with Mukha ng Langit, a leaping maneuver that branches into cross-up strikes and behind-the-back mix-ups.

---

## Bayani Mode Alters Frame Advantage and Drive Gauge Management

Yasmine's central offensive engine revolves around Bayani Mode, an install state that modifies her standard special attacks. Activating this state alters her frame advantage on block, transforming move sequences that are normally unsafe into sustained pressure traps.

This install requirement forces players to balance Drive Gauge consumption alongside special mode activation. Using enhanced versions of her special moves speeds up combo execution, but burning through meter too early leaves her vulnerable to Drive Reversal counters.

---

## Defensive Reversals and High-Level Competitive Outlook

Defensively, Yasmine features startup invincibility on her Overdrive Lipad ng Agila as well as her Super Arts. Having a reliable invincible reversal ensures she can contest aggressive corner pressure, a critical requirement for high-level viable fighters in Street Fighter 6.

Her reliance on resource management and ambiguous setup traps positions her as a strong option for players specializing in technical trickster archetypes. As tournament competitors prepare to incorporate her into active rosters, testing will determine how her blockstrings hold up against established top-tier defenders.
