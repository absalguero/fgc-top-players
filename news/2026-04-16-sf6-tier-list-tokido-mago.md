Japanese FGC legends Tokido, Mago, and Gachikun have consolidated the Street Fighter 6 S-tier down to just Ed, JP, and Sagat in their latest character ranking. This tight grouping at the top reflects a solidified meta following the March update featuring Alex. Our tracking data at [FGC Top Players](https://fgctopplayers.com/character-stats/) often captures wide character spreads in tournament top eights. This veteran assessment highlights a strict and unforgiving upper echelon instead.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **S-Tier Shrinks:** Only Ed, JP, and Sagat make the cut for the top rank.
* **Perfect Parry Penalties:** Defensive system changes continue to drop characters without invincible reversals down the list.
* **Newcomer Struggles:** The newest addition to the roster, Alex, debuts at the very bottom in E-tier.

</div>

---

## Defensive Liabilities and System Adjustments

The panel discussion centered heavily on offensive power and defensive liabilities. The perfect parry changes introduced earlier this year remain a defining factor in character viability. Characters lacking reliable invincible reversals are getting punished hard by the current system mechanics. This shift is the primary reason A.K.I. has dropped out of favor and landed firmly in the C-tier.

> **"A.K.I.'s offense is so strong that her defense was left weak, and with the Perfect Parry adjustments, she's become significantly weaker."**
> — *Tokido, Professional Player*

---
