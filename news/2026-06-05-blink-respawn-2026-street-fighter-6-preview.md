## The Gauntlet: The Path to Capcom Cup

Blink Respawn holds significant weight in the current circuit. Offline Premier events provide a direct auto-qualification spot to Capcom Cup for the tournament winner. The stakes extend beyond circuit points. Players will fight for a share of an $11,200 total prize pool. The champion takes home $5,600 along with the coveted finals ticket.

## The Favorites: Hometown Heroes and Touring Champions

Yamaguchi stands out as a primary target for the rest of the field. His Evo Japan victory cemented his status at the top of the competitive hierarchy. He currently holds the number one rank on FGC Top Players. His precise execution makes him incredibly difficult to eliminate in a best of five format.

MenaRD enters the tournament as a major favorite. Competing in front of a passionate Santo Domingo crowd adds a unique variable to his path. Punk arrives with a strong track record of deep tournament runs throughout the spring season. He relies on fundamental mastery and precise whiff punishment to dismantle challengers. Tokido and Gachikun round out the veteran presence.

## The Disruptors: Technical Specialists and Grappler Threats

EndingWalker commands attention with his highly optimized Ed play. He maximizes the character's long-range tools and high-damage output to dictate the pace of neutral. His defensive discipline makes him a severe threat to any established veteran in the bracket.

Kobayan brings the terrifying presence of Zangief into the international spotlight. He secured a top placement at Combo Breaker 2026 against a fierce field. Grappler specialists thrive in high-stakes environments where a single read shifts the momentum of an entire set. Navigating a dense open bracket will test his consistency against unfamiliar international opponents.

## The Pick: Forecasting the Champion

The clash between veteran consistency and rising talent shapes the narrative for this weekend. Winning requires rapid adaptation to the local environment and the stamina to survive an open bracket. 

Punk will win Blink Respawn 2026. He possesses the momentum from consistent recent performances and the execution required to navigate this specific field. His flawless neutral game directly counters the aggressive strategies favored by competitors like MenaRD. He will methodically break down his challengers to secure the Capcom Cup spot.
