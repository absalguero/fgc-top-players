**Sahara** has won Capcom Cup 12 on Saturday and secured the $1,000,000 grand prize. His victory marks the culmination of an intensely competitive 2025-2026 Capcom Pro Tour season.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **An Unprecedented Bracket Run**: Sahara tore through the top 16 with dominant victories over seasoned veterans to claim the ultimate prize.

## The Improbable Million-Dollar Run

The global Fighting Game Community tuned in to watch the thrilling conclusion of the Capcom Pro Tour season at the Ryogoku Kokugikan in Tokyo, Japan. The arena was packed with passionate fans eager to see history unfold. Millions more watched the live broadcasts online. The atmosphere was electric as the top 16 players battled for supremacy. Sahara emerged victorious to claim the Capcom Cup 12 championship. The tournament featured a staggering $1,282,000 total prize pool. 

Sahara's top 16 bracket run was an absolute masterclass in fighting game fundamentals. Sahara played Ed throughout the tournament and showed why he's considered the best character in the game. He demonstrated perfect spacing and incredible mental fortitude throughout the final day. He started by sweeping Xerna with a flawless 5-0 victory. He then outpaced Dual Kevin in a tense 5-3 set. Next, he defeated Blaz with another strong 5-3 performance to reach the grand finals. In the ultimate showdown, Sahara completely dominated kilzyou to win the final match 5-1 and secure the tournament victory. His execution under immense pressure solidified his reputation as a true champion.
