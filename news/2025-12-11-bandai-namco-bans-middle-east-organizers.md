**Bandai Namco Esports** has issued a permanent ban to specific tournament organizers in the **MEA3 region** today. An official statement confirmed that these organizers violated the Tekken World Tour 2025 official rules by "encouraging collusive behavior intended to secure a specific player’s victory."

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Permanent Ban:** The organizers have been permanently banned from all Bandai Namco Entertainment eSports circuits, including the Tekken World Tour and EMEA Tekken Cup.
* **Points Revoked:** All Dojo events operated by these organizers will have their points revoked.
* **Specific Violation:** Documentation submitted to the administration proved the organizers encouraged collusion to help a specific player win.

---

## "Clear, Objective, and Credible Evidence"

In a statement released today, Bandai Namco Esports confirmed that "documentation supporting these allegations" was submitted to the TWT administration. The publisher emphasized that because the Dojo system is a "trust-based framework," any disciplinary action must be grounded in "clear, objective, and credible evidence."

The investigation found that the organizers in the MEA3 region were guilty of encouraging collusive behavior. The statement explicitly noted that this behavior was intended to "secure a specific player’s victory," a direct violation of the tour's integrity.

> **"As a result, all Dojo events operated by these organizers will have their points revoked, and the organizers have been permanently banned from participating in any Bandai Namco Entertainment eSports circuits."**
> — *Bandai Namco Esports Official Announcement*

---

## Immediate Impact on Leaderboards

The sanctions include the retroactive removal of points from all Dojo events run by the banned organizers. This decision will immediately affect the standings for the region. Bandai Namco has explicitly asked all players registered in the MEA3 region to **"review their current placement on the regional leaderboard at their earliest convenience."**

**Scope of the Ban:**
* **Tekken World Tour:** Permanent exclusion.
* **EMEA Tekken Cup:** Permanent exclusion.
* **Future Participation:** Banned from participating in "any Bandai Namco Entertainment eSports circuits."

---

## Future Moderation Updates

Bandai Namco acknowledged that "concerns about potential misconduct have arisen in multiple regions in the past." However, the publisher reiterated that action can only be taken when concerns are supported by credible evidence.

Moving forward, the company stated they will continue "refining our moderation practices and updating regulations where necessary" to reduce the likelihood of misconduct and improve the competitive environment.
