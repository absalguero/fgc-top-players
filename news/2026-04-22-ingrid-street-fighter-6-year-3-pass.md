The extradimensional peacekeeper Ingrid showcased her modern moveset in a new gameplay trailer, previewing the next upcoming addition to the Street Fighter 6 roster.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Ingrid's gameplay was revealed** ahead of her official release.
* **Her magic-based attacks return**, featuring cosmic projectiles and energy-based juggle combos.
* **Her space-control tools** offer a distinct contrast to the current cast of strike-throw specialists.

</div>

---

## A Magic-Based Arsenal

Her upcoming arrival on May 28 brings her signature magical attacks and cosmic energy to the modern engine. The trailer confirms her seamless visual transition into the RE Engine. She maintains her traditional purple dress and glowing crests.

The updated graphical style adds a heavy layer of particle effects to every attack. Her gameplay trailer features a moveset heavily inspired by her previous iterations. She relies on elegant, magic-based strikes and spatial manipulation to control the screen. In the new footage, Ingrid effortlessly juggles Ryu and JP with glowing glyphs, rapid spin kicks, and devastating cosmic projectiles. When executing her Super Arts, she traps opponents in celestial rings before delivering localized cosmic blasts.

---

## Exploring New Spacing Strategies

The introduction of a fighter with strong space-control magic could force a shift in how top players approach the neutral game. Competitors who thrive on dictating screen real estate will need to spend serious hours testing her frame data and juggle potential once she drops. Ingrid brings a completely different flavor to a cast currently dominated by grounded, strike-throw specialists.
