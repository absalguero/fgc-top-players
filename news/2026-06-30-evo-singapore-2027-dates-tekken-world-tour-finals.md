The first-ever Evo Singapore is bringing the Tekken World Tour Finals along with it to Marina Bay Sands.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Evo Singapore lands at Marina Bay Sands:** The tournament takes place January 15-17, 2027.
* **Tekken World Tour integration:** The 2026 Tekken World Tour Finals will run concurrently at the same venue.
* **A historic shift for Bandai Namco:** This event marks the first time a Tekken World Tour season concludes at an Evo event.

</div>

---

## Evo Singapore Locks In Dates and Location

The Evolution Championship Series revealed details for its inaugural Singapore event during Evo 2026 on June 28, 2026. Fighting game fans can expect the main bracket action to unfold from January 15-17, 2027. The chosen venue is Marina Bay Sands. This lands premier competitive fighting games directly in Southeast Asia.

This expansion signals a structural change for global esports circuits. Evo General Manager Rick Thiher confirmed that the tournament will serve as a double feature. Instead of running as a separate standalone circuit finale, the 2026 Tekken World Tour Finals will share the venue.

---

## Tekken World Tour Finals Integration

The collaboration brings a completely unique format to the competitive Tekken 8 landscape. Group stages for the championship will begin on January 14, 2027. This schedule places the early matches just one day prior to the open Evo brackets. Competitors will fight through group play and the Last Chance Qualifier before culminating in the grand finals on the main stage. 

Bringing the conclusion of a circuit directly into the Evo ecosystem is unprecedented for Bandai Namco. Typically, the Tekken World Tour wraps up at independent venues across the globe. By aligning with Evo Singapore, organizers are concentrating top-tier international talent into a single weekend block.

---

## The Shifting Landscape of Global Esports

This announcement follows a broader trend of international growth for the fighting game scene. The dual tournament layout guarantees that Singapore will be the focal point of the community at the start of 2027. Players will need to balance open bracket runs alongside watching the absolute highest level of professional Tekken play.

More details regarding secondary game lineups, open registration dates, and ticket sales will arrive as the event approaches.
