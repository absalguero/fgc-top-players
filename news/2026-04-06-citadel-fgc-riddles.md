## Roster Acquisition

Citadel Gaming acquired Michael "Riddles" Kim for its fighting game division. The organization broadcast the signing using the #EnterTheCitadel tag via social media. This move ends his period of independent competition. He brings high-level execution to a brand actively building its footprint across multiple esports titles.

---

## The Competitor's Pedigree

Riddles built a fearsome reputation piloting traditional fighting game characters in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. His optimization of Terry Bogard and Kazuya Mishima led to major tournament victories at events like The Big House 10 and Collision 2023. He spent multiple years representing Team Liquid. Following his departure from the brand, he operated as an independent competitor. He stepped back from competitive Super Smash Bros. Ultimate after Kagaribi 13 to dedicate his focus toward Street Fighter 6. This success across different fighting game genres makes him a compelling anchor for an expanding esports organization.

---

## Citadel Market Position

Citadel Gaming is an esports organization fielding competitive rosters in League of Legends, Marvel Rivals, and Teamfight Tactics. The brand is now building a fighting game lineup designed to threaten the late stages of major international events. Survival on the modern tournament circuit demands premium talent. Bringing Riddles on board provides a vital structural foundation for the team. This roster move establishes Citadel as a serious contender fielding proven champions.
