**Gen.G Esports** has officially expanded its Fighting Game Community division, confirming the signing of Korean Tekken competitor Jaegyun "Mulgold" Han.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Gen.G acquires Mulgold, one of Korea's premier Tekken talents.**
* **Mulgold is known for his world-class Claudio and Feng gameplay.**
* **The move reinforces Gen.G's commitment to the Tekken 8 competitive ecosystem.**

---

## Gen.G Enters the Iron Fist Tournament

Global esports powerhouse **Gen.G** has strengthened its foothold in fighting games by signing **Jaegyun "Mulgold" Han** to its *Tekken* division. The organization announced the move on social media, welcoming the decorated competitor to their squad as they look to capitalize on the thriving *Tekken 8* circuit.

Mulgold has long been a staple of the Korean *Tekken* scene, earning a reputation for his patient yet explosive playstyle. Best known as a loyal **Claudio** specialist who also pilots a formidable **Feng**, Mulgold has consistently placed in the top bracket of international majors. His resume includes a recent victory at **Combo Breaker 2025** and a strong top-8 finish at **EVO 2025**, proving he remains one of the most dangerous bracket threats in the world.

By picking up a player of Mulgold's caliber, Gen.G signals that they are not just participating in the *Tekken World Tour* but aiming to win it. With this signing, Mulgold becomes the primary face of Gen.G's *Tekken* efforts. The organization has a history of supporting top-tier Korean talent across multiple titles, and this move aligns with their strategy of securing proven winners. This acquisition provides the organization with an immediate contender capable of challenging the dominance of rival squads like DRX.
