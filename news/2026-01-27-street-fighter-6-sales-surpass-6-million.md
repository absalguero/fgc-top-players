**Street Fighter 6** has officially **reached 6.36 million units sold** as of December 31, 2025, confirming the fighting game's continued dominance in the global market.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Steady Growth:** The title sold an impressive 601,000 units between October and December 2025.
* **Fiscal Success:** Capcom reported net sales of 115.3 billion yen for the third quarter, a 29.8% increase year-over-year.
* **Hegemon Status:** Sales data indicates *Street Fighter 6* is currently outperforming internal expectations, maintaining high momentum nearly three years post-launch.

---

## Capcom Financials Reveal SF6 Momentum

Capcom released its consolidated financial results for the third quarter of the fiscal year ending March 2026. The report highlights that *Street Fighter 6* has achieved a cumulative sales total of 6.36 million units as of December 31, 2025. This marks a significant increase of approximately 601,000 units sold during the three-month period from October to December.

The publisher's overall performance remains strong, with operating income reaching 54.3 billion yen. This figure represents a 75.1% increase compared to the same period last year. The continued success of *Street Fighter 6* has established it as a primary revenue driver for the company, with sales figures that suggest it is competing closely with, or even outpacing, other flagship franchises like *Monster Hunter* in recent quarterly performance.
