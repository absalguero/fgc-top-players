AG Global signed top Chinese *Street Fighter 6* competitors Vxbao and Zhen to secure Club Championship points at the 2026 Esports World Cup. The organization announced the FGC roster expansion on social media. The move guarantees top-tier backing for the two free agents ahead of the international tournament.

## EWC Club Championship Strategy

The Esports World Cup rewards organizations that field competitive rosters across multiple game titles. Points from individual top-eight tournament finishes contribute to a multimillion-dollar overall club prize pool. Organizations must diversify their competitive portfolios to climb the global leaderboard. Tournament rules dictate that only the single highest placement per game counts toward a club's total score. Securing two established threats in *Street Fighter 6* doubles the organization's chances of surviving the bracket and cashing in on those required top-eight points.

---

## Expanding the All Gamers Footprint

All Gamers is a prominent Chinese esports brand with a legacy in team shooters and mobile MOBAs. The organization dominates regional circuits in CrossFire and Honor of Kings. Their aggressive expansion into the fighting game community illustrates the broader economic impact of the EWC structure. The tournament ecosystem incentivizes legacy brands to step outside their traditional genres. Vxbao and Zhen provide immediate tournament viability. Both competitors possess the high-level match experience required to navigate an international bracket. They will wear the AG Global colors when the competition begins in Riyadh.
