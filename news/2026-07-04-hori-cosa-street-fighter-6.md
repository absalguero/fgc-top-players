Street Fighter 6 competitor cosa has secured a brand partnership with peripheral manufacturer HORI to back his global competitive season.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* Saishunkan Sol Kumamoto competitor cosa has partnered with hardware manufacturer HORI.
* The professional player uses HORI's Fighting Commander OCTA controller for tournament execution.
* HORI will provide hardware support for international travels alongside joint marketing campaigns.

</div>

---

## Dedicated Hardware Support for the Kumamoto Competitor

The collaboration brings together one of Japan's rising fighting game talents and an influential hardware manufacturer. Representing Saishunkan Sol Kumamoto, cosa has established a reputation for high-level execution on a traditional controller layout. HORI confirmed that the competitor relies on the Fighting Commander OCTA, a specialized six-button pad built specifically for fighting games, as his primary input device.

Through this agreement, the manufacturer will provide hardware resources to aid the player during his international tournament travel. The company also announced plans to launch joint promotional initiatives featuring the player, expanding their footprint within the active esports scene.

---

## A Legacy of Fighting Game Hardware Innovation

Founded in 1969, HORI has spent decades producing tournament-grade peripherals that shaped how players interact with fighting games. For arcade stick enthusiasts, the company is synonymous with legacy lines like the Real Arcade Pro series and the modern Fighting Stick Alpha. 

The manufacturer also recognized early on that a significant portion of the community prefers controllers over joysticks. The Fighting Commander line was created to serve this niche, offering a layout that places six face buttons within easy reach. By supporting a modern professional like cosa, the brand maintains its historical role of equipping top competitors with dedicated equipment.

---

## Upcoming Circuit Implications and Peripheral Visibility

Reliable hardware remains an essential factor for professional players dealing with rigorous international travel schedules. HORI intends to use this sponsorship to highlight their specialized fightpads on major tournament broadcasts. Fans can expect to see the manufacturer's branding alongside the player during high-stakes league matches and international majors later this year.
