**Zhen and Vxbao** have officially partnered with **8BitDo** today, confirming the release of a new "Signature Edition" Arcade Stick designed for the Fighting Game Community.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **New Hardware:** 8BitDo reveals a Signature Edition Arcade Stick featuring branding from the Chinese duo.
* **Pricing & Release:** The controller is listed at **$94.99** with a release date of **January 31, 2026**.
* **Tech Specs:** The device features tri-mode connectivity (Bluetooth, 2.4G, Wired) and a hot-swappable, mod-friendly design.

---

## 8BitDo Taps Chinese Powerhouses

In a move solidifying the bridge between competitive performance and peripheral design, 8BitDo announced the collaboration today. The partnership leverages the massive popularity of **Zhen** and **Vxbao**, who have both been instrumental in **KuaiShou Gaming's** recent *Street Fighter League* success.

The centerpiece of this collaboration is a special edition of the 8BitDo Arcade Stick. Differentiating itself from the standard lineup, this model sports a striking "Transparent Purple" chassis. It retains the line's signature **Dynamic Button Layout** where the visual button labels change depending on whether the user is in "Switch" or "PC" mode. While competitive *Street Fighter 6* is primarily played on PC and PS5, the inclusion of Switch compatibility is a staple of 8BitDo's engineering, allowing the stick to function as a versatile controller for retro games and modern Nintendo titles alike.

---

## Details & Additional Information

The **8BitDo Arcade Stick - Signature Edition** is built on the company's popular mod-friendly chassis, designed to let enthusiasts easily swap out standard components for Sanwa or Seimitsu parts if desired. It supports three distinct connectivity modes including Bluetooth, 2.4G wireless, and wired USB-C, ensuring low-latency performance for tournament play while maintaining wireless convenience for casual use.

According to retail listings, the controller will retail for **$94.99** and is scheduled to ship on **January 31, 2026**.
