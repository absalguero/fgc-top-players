**Kobayan** has secured a permanent transfer to Saishunkan Sol Kumamoto, confirming his ongoing commitment to the prominent Japanese Street Fighter League roster.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Permanent Roster Spot:** The Zangief specialist transitions from a temporary loan player to a full team member.
* **Effective April 1st:** The full transfer from his previous organization, Answer.M.Gaming, becomes active at the start of the new month.
* **Legacy Continues:** Saishunkan Sol Kumamoto pledged to carry forward the competitive spirit of his former team.

---

## From Loan to Full-Time Member

The Japanese esports organization Saishunkan Sol Kumamoto announced a permanent roster update regarding their Street Fighter 6 division. Following a dominant run as a loan player, the celebrated Zangief loyalist Kobayan is making his stay permanent. This move solidifies the team's lineup as they prepare for the upcoming competitive season.

The transfer involves an agreement with Answer.M.Gaming. Kobayan had been competing under the SSK banner on a temporary basis for recent league events. The organization expressed deep respect for A.M.G during the transition and promised to honor their legacy moving forward.

Kobayan took the FGC by storm after a dominant display at the Topanga Championship, firmly establishing himself as one of the premier Zangief specialists in the world. He has consistently placed high in major tournaments, including third-place finishes at EVO Japan 2025 and EVO France 2025. His precise input execution and devastating reads have made him a fan favorite and a terrifying opponent in the competitive scene.

---

## Strengthening the SFL Contenders

Saishunkan Sol Kumamoto has been a formidable presence in the Street Fighter League Pro-JP. They have consistently reached the playoff stages, notably securing third-place finishes in both the 2023 and 2024 seasons. Entering their next campaign, the team is hungry to capture their first-ever SFL championship.

The team's current roster is anchored by the veteran leadership of team captain Nemo, alongside the highly experienced Machabo and the skilled shoto specialist cosa. With Sasamo recently joining as their coach, the organization has built a well-rounded and strategic unit. Acquiring Kobayan on a full transfer ensures that one of the strongest grappler players remains a cornerstone of this top-tier franchise. Fans of the team can breathe easy knowing the roster retains its heavy hitter as new bracket runs begin.
