Gunma's legendary Dudley main and current Street Fighter 6 Kimberly specialist MATSU56 has joined the LaVISION esports roster.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **LaVISION bolsters its Street Fighter 6 competitive lineup by signing top Japanese talent MATSU56.**
* **The competitor brings his legacy as "Gunma's strongest Dudley" into the modern era.**
* **He currently competes at the highest level utilizing Kimberly's aggressive toolkit.**

</div>

---

## Expanding the FGC Roster

The Japanese esports organization announced the acquisition of MATSU56 to serve as a core pillar for their fighting game division. LaVISION operates with a distinct mission to bridge the gap between top-tier professionals and high-level challengers in the scene. Securing a veteran competitor aligns perfectly with their goal of expanding the competitive base. Representatives noted the addition of such a central talent pushes their team to its next stage of development.

Adding a dedicated Kimberly player diversifies the competitive representation for the squad. MATSU56 built a strong regional reputation in the Ultra Street Fighter 4 era through his precise Dudley execution on Japanese arcade leaderboards. He has since adapted his aggressive rushdown style to the Street Fighter 6 engine. His established track record at global tournaments made him a prime target for the growing organization.

---

## Adapting to the Modern Meta

Organizational backing fundamentally changes a competitor's ceiling. MATSU56 now possesses the travel resources needed to target international stops on the Capcom Pro Tour. His top 100 placement at Evo Japan 2025 proved his offensive gameplan scales to major brackets. LaVISION clearly invested in that exact momentum.

With Dudley absent from the Street Fighter 6 roster, MATSU56 found a natural successor for his aggressive playstyle in Kimberly. She allows him to maintain his historic emphasis on corner pressure and forced defensive errors. Now that LaVISION is handling travel logistics, he can dedicate all his energy toward optimizing this rushdown strategy against global talent.
