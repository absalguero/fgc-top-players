The wait is over for Street Fighter 6 fans across the continent. Street Fighter League (SFL) Pro-Europe 2025 officially launches today, **November 22, 2025**, marking the start of Season 4. This season introduces a condensed, high-stakes format featuring six elite organizations battling for the European title. With a revamped lineup of competitors, including historic debuts and returning champions, the race to the Street Fighter League World Championship 2025 promises to be more intense than ever.

## Condensed Offline Schedule
Unlike previous marathon seasons, the 2025 edition is delivering a lightning-fast, action-packed week of competition. The tournament runs from **November 22 through November 29, 2025**.

Hosted offline, the league will test team synergy and individual skill in a rapid-fire environment. Every match is critical in this shorter window, as teams vie for the final playoff seeding. The winning squad will claim the title of European Champions and secure their place on the global stage to face the champions from the US and Japan leagues at the World Championship.

## Official Team Rosters
This year’s lineup features a mix of European powerhouses and exciting new challengers. Notable additions include the league’s first-ever African team, **Goliath Gaming**, and the debut of French fan-favorites, **Solary**.

The confirmed squads competing for the title include:

* **Ninjas in Pyjamas (NIP):** The defending champions return with a "super team" roster featuring **Phenom**, **Juicyjoe**, **AngryBird**, and **Big Bird**.
* **MOUZ:** A consistent threat in the region, blending veteran experience with young talent in **Problem X**, **EndingWalker**, **Garnet**, and **Lexx**.
* **AEGIS:** A tactically dangerous squad featuring some of France’s most technical players: **Mister Crimson**, **Kilzyou**, **Valmaster**, and **Gamein99**.
* **Wolves Esports:** A diverse roster known for high-level execution with **Hurricane**, **Takamura**, **Kusanagi**, and **Infexious**.
* **Solary:** Making their SFL debut, the French organization fields a formidable roster anchored by French talent **HeyyPepito**, **Akainu**, and **Senor Power**, strengthened by the addition of top Saudi competitor **vWsym**.
* **Goliath Gaming:** Making history as the first African team to compete in the European league with **JabhiM**, **ChuX**, **JoKeR JoKeZ**, and **MarkTheShark**.

## Opening Matches (Episode 1)
The season kicks off immediately with high-profile matchups. The "Episode 1" schedule pits the newcomers directly against established veterans, setting the tone for the week.

* **Match 1:** Ninjas in Pyjamas vs. Solary
* **Match 2:** Goliath Gaming vs. MOUZ
* **Match 3:** Wolves Esports vs. AEGIS
