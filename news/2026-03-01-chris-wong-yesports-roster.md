**Chris Wong** has officially signed with Yesports, confirming a massive roster addition for the Street Fighter 6 competitive scene.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Chris Wong is officially returning to the Yesports organization.**
* **He will be teaming up with longtime FGC veterans HotDog29, Rainpro, Humanbomb, and Keekuma.**
* **This signing significantly strengthens the Yesports presence in the competitive scene.**

---

## Return to Former Sponsor

Chris Wong announced his return to his former organizational home to his followers online. He expressed his enthusiasm for reuniting with the team and playing alongside his colleagues. The move brings a massive powerhouse competitor back under the Yesports banner. 

Chris Wong is widely known for his phenomenal bracket runs and top-tier character execution. He made waves globally with his dominant Luke gameplay during his incredible run to the Capcom Cup X grand finals. His presence automatically elevates the organization's standing in upcoming major tournaments. 

---

## The Yesports Lineup

The Yesports Street Fighter 6 division features several notable players. The organization has secured a formidable lineup of talent to tackle the current competitive circuit. The squad includes Chris Wong alongside highly respected competitors HotDog29 and Rainpro. Humanbomb and Keekuma round out the roster to create a highly capable team. 

This specific group represents some of the strongest talent within the Hong Kong fighting game community. Their combined tournament experience and matchup knowledge make them a significant threat in any global bracket.

---

## Competitive Impact

Fans will be eager to see how this fortified squad performs in the upcoming season. The FGC will undoubtedly keep a close eye on their progress in international brackets. The gathering of these high-level players under one banner also positions them perfectly as a unit for any future team-based events.
