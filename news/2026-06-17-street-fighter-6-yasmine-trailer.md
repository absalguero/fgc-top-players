Yasmine drops into the Street Fighter 6 roster with a brand-new Eskrima moveset and karambit knife in just a few weeks.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Yasmine makes her playable debut on August 3.**
* **Her kit centers around high-speed Eskrima strikes and an install state called Bayani Mode.**
* **A global Battle Balance patch will drop alongside her release.**

</div>

---

## Eskrima Kit & Bayani Mode Explained

Capcom has pulled the curtain back on the first fighter of the Year 4 roster. Yasmine features a relentless rushdown style grounded in real-world Eskrima and Silat martial arts. She controls the mid-range space with her crescent-shaped karambit knife before closing gaps using tools like her swirling projectile, Pangil sa Likuran. To bypass neutral entirely, she relies on a front-flip mixup called Mukha ng Langit that threatens both frontal strikes and spinning back hits.

The core of her gameplan revolves around entering Bayani Mode. This powered-up state unlocks new pressure sequences and is triggered by landing specific strikes, including her Talim ng Hangin low sweep or the multi-slice Alon follow-up. Her Level 2 Super Art, Nakatagong Lakas, acts as an extended install, keeping her locked into Bayani Mode for a set duration while granting access to unique dash-behind attacks. 

---

## Year 4 Meta Disruption

The Street Fighter 6 Year 4 Character Pass and Ultimate Pass are now live for purchase. Players securing the pass will have Yasmine unlocked the moment her August release date arrives in both her default combat attire and her unlockable school uniform. 

Her highly mobile archetype and install-based pressure promise to introduce new variables into the competitive meta. The constant threat of Bayani Mode means opponents will be forced to respect her offense or risk getting snowballed by enhanced specials and corner carry routes. Crucially, Yasmine arrives alongside a roster-wide Battle Balance update. The entire cast will be adjusting to a shifted tier list at the exact moment a high-speed, projectile-wielding rushdown character enters the fray.
