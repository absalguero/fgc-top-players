Arc System Works has officially confirmed the location for the ARC World Tour 2025-2026 Finals. The tour’s latest trailer reveals that the championship event will move to Seoul, South Korea. This marks a significant shift as the finals leave the United States for the first time in history.

## A Historic Move to Seoul
The event is scheduled for March 20–22, 2026. It will take place at the KINTEX Exhibition Hall 2, Hall 6. This change brings the 10th anniversary of the tour to a major global esports hub. The top qualifiers will compete in *Guilty Gear -Strive-*, *Granblue Fantasy Versus: Rising*, and *Under Night In-Birth II Sys:Celes*.

## Ticket Information & VIP Perks
Arc System Works announced that general admission to the finals is completely free. Fans can attend the event and watch the matches without purchasing a ticket. 

For attendees seeking a premium experience, **VIP passes** are available. The Early Bird VIP All-Day Pass is priced at 28,000 KRW, while on-site VIP tickets will cost 35,000 KRW. The VIP pass includes several exclusive benefits:
* **VIP Welcome Kit:** A collection of exclusive merchandise for pass holders.
* **Priority Access:** Skip the lines for various events and experience booths throughout the venue.
* **Special Live Access:** Priority viewing rights for the "ARC WORLD TOUR Special Live in KOREA" performance.

## A Global Celebration
This relocation to Seoul signifies a new chapter for the Arc World Tour as it celebrates its 10th anniversary. By bringing the finals to Asia, Arc System Works is acknowledging the global passion for their titles and setting the stage for an unforgettable showdown. With the "Heat" rising and champions from around the world gathering, this promises to be the most competitive and electric finale yet.
