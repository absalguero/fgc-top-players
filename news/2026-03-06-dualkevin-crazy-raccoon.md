**DualKevin** has officially signed with Crazy Raccoon on Friday, confirming a major international roster expansion for the legendary Japanese esports organization.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Top Tier Talent:** The North American standout brings his impressive resume and current global rank of #6 on FGC Top Players to the renowned Japanese roster.
* **International Expansion:** Crazy Raccoon continues to broaden its global footprint by recruiting top-level competitors from outside of Japan.
* **Road to the Cup:** The sponsorship arrives at a critical moment as the community gears up for Capcom Cup 12 and the next competitive season.

---

## International Roster Expansion

The prominent Japanese esports organization announced the addition of the talented player to their competitive lineup on Friday. Crazy Raccoon shared the news with fans via their official X account. This roster move adds another formidable competitor to their dedicated Street Fighter division. The team already boasts an impressive legacy with FGC veterans like Dogura, Kazunoko, Bonchan, and Shuto. 

Securing a sponsorship with a premier team provides essential backing for extensive tournament travel. DualKevin is widely recognized for his high-level play with Rashid. He has consistently placed at the top of competitive brackets across multiple titles over the years. The veteran competitor currently sits at rank #6 in the world on FGC Top Players. The fighting game community reacted enthusiastically to the new partnership as it bridges North American talent with one of Japan's most popular esports brands.

---

## Capcom Cup 12 and the 2026 Season

Competitors are actively preparing for the most prestigious events of the year. The backing of a major esports organization gives players the resources needed to focus entirely on their gameplay and bracket runs. Fans can expect to see the CR tag next to his name in all future tournament appearances. 

The immediate focus for the newly sponsored player will be his highly anticipated appearance at Capcom Cup 12. He officially qualified for the massive event after a grueling World Warrior campaign. The Street Fighter 6 competitive landscape continues to evolve rapidly. This high-profile signing positions Crazy Raccoon as a dominant international force heading into the 2026 Street Fighter League season and beyond.
