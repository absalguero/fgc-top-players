Street Fighter League JAPAN's 2026 season now has a full calendar, and it stretches well into next year. Capcom Fighters confirmed the schedule, with the campaign opening in August and running all the way to a Grand Final in February 2027.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Division Showdown opens the season August 10-11, 2026**
* **Regular season play begins August 25, 2026**
* **Playoffs and the Grand Final push the campaign into early 2027, closing February 23**

</div>

---

## SFL JAPAN 2026 Calendar Set

The season kicks off with the Division Showdown on August 10 and 11, 2026, before the regular season officially begins on August 25. From there, teams will grind through league play for months before the format shifts to playoffs on January 10 and 11, 2027. The Grand Final caps the season on February 23, 2027.

That span, from a mid-August showdown to a late-February final, gives SFL JAPAN one of the longer competitive windows on the Street Fighter 6 calendar. Teams will need to manage roster form and player health across nearly seven months of competition rather than a compressed few weeks.

## What the Schedule Means for Teams

With the Division Showdown less than a month out, SFL JAPAN rosters have a tight window to lock in preparation before the format that determines seeding takes shape. The long gap between the August kickoff and the January playoffs also means squads will need to sustain form over an extended stretch, rather than peaking for a short tournament run.
