## Tournament Returns to French Riviera with Fresh Roster

Evo France 2026 is returning to Nice this October with an eight-game main stage lineup highlighted by the premier tournament presence of Marvel Tōkon: Fighting Souls. Following a successful inaugural European run last year, the championship series solidifies its place on the international circuit. The three-day event will run from October 9 through October 11, 2026.

Registration opened on June 28, 2026, giving players ample time to lock in their travel arrangements. While established staples dominate the schedule, the inclusion of unreleased and newly launched software changes the preparation dynamic for top competitors. A single main stage will host all final brackets throughout the weekend.

---

## Late-Season Circuit Implications and Regional Defense

The autumn placement positions the French event as a critical stop for players looking to secure late-season points. European talent will defend the home bracket against international rosters traveling from North America and Asia. With the strategies for games like 2XKO and Fatal Fury already highly optimized by veteran players, the addition of a brand-new title like Marvel Tōkon adds an element of unpredictability to the entire weekend.
