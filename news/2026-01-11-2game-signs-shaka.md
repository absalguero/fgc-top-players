## 2Game Continues Expansion

2Game Esports has made another major splash in the Fighting Game Community by signing **Shaka**, one of South America's most respected *Street Fighter* competitors. Known for his mastery of Dhalsim, Shaka brings years of high-level experience to the Brazilian organization, having previously qualified for prestigious events like Capcom Cup 10.

The announcement comes as 2Game Esports aggressively strengthens its position in the *Street Fighter 6* ecosystem. Shaka joins a roster that has seen significant growth over the last few months, with the organization picking up top-tier talent to compete on the global stage. He will be fighting alongside teammates such as **Blaz** and **Tashi**, both of whom have already secured their spots for Capcom Cup 12. 

This signing highlights 2Game's strategy of consolidating the best talent from the LATAM region, creating a "super team" capable of challenging the world's best at upcoming major tournaments.
