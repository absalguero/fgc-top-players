**Bandits Gaming** has officially signed Street Fighter 6 competitor **EnzoTheHokage** to their roster, the organization confirmed in a post on their official X account.

**Key Takeaways:**
* **EnzoTheHokage joins Bandits Gaming as their newest Street Fighter 6 talent, a Ken specialist who has recently expanded his arsenal to include Sagat.**
* **The signing underscores Bandits' continued investment in building out their SF6 presence heading into a new CPT season.**
* **With Capcom Cup 12 already set, Enzo and Bandits have their eyes firmly on competing for a spot in the next Capcom Cup cycle.**

---

## Bandits Gaming Add a Rising SF6 Talent to Their Lineup

Bandits Gaming confirmed the addition of EnzoTheHokage to their Street Fighter 6 roster via an official post on X. The organization framed the signing around two clear goals: continued player development and high-level competition at every tournament stop on the circuit.

EnzoTheHokage built his FGC reputation as a Ken player, using the character's rushdown pressure and combo flexibility to grind deep into brackets on the competitive circuit. In recent months he has also picked up Sagat, adding the Emperor of Muay Thai's long-range Tiger Shot zoning and punishing Tiger Uppercut to his toolkit. That kind of character range signals a player who is actively evolving his game and thinking seriously about tournament adaptability.

His most notable result to date came at EVO 2025 in Las Vegas, where he competed against a stacked field of thousands of entrants and finished in the top 49. Placing that deep in a bracket featuring some of the world's best SF6 players confirmed Enzo as a legitimate competitive threat beyond the regional level. Landing on an established organization like Bandits Gaming now gives him the institutional support, resources, and exposure to take the next step in his career.

---

## Eyes on the Next Capcom Pro Tour Season

With Capcom Cup 12 running from March 11 through March 15, 2026 at Ryogoku Kokugikan in Tokyo and the 48-player field already locked in, this signing is squarely about positioning for what comes next. The upcoming CPT season represents a clean slate for the entire field, and Bandits Gaming is making clear they intend to have competitive representation in the hunt for qualification points from the jump.

For a player of Enzo's profile, competing under an org banner changes the equation considerably. Sponsorship brings travel support, coaching resources, and the day-to-day stability needed to grind a full CPT calendar without the logistical burdens that hold back many independent competitors. Every Regional Premier and Online Premier on the 2026 schedule becomes an opportunity to stack points toward Capcom Cup 13 qualification.

Bandits Gaming has framed this as a long-term investment in growth alongside immediate competitive expectations. With a dual-character threat in Ken and Sagat, and organizational backing behind him, EnzoTheHokage enters the new season with a legitimate platform to make noise on the CPT circuit.
