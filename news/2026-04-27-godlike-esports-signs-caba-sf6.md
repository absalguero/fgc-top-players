GodLike Esports secured an active competitor for the Street Fighter 6 bracket at the Esports World Cup by signing Caba right before the roster lock.

<div class="takeaways">

**Key Takeaways:**
* **Roster Expansion:** GodLike Esports enters the fighting game community by signing top-ranked Dominican player Caba.
* **Team Strategy:** The organization aims to win the team prize by fielding representation across all EWC games.
* **Competitive Pedigree:** Caba brings proven momentum to the roster following a standout podium finish at Frosty Faustings XVIII 2026.

</div>

---

## The Club Championship Meta

GodLike Esports announced the signing of Caba on social media to confirm their expansion into the fighting game community. The Indian organization noted their intention to bring "heat to every single fight" alongside the roster update. 

The move is a calculated business decision driven by the Club Championship program. The organization is actively trying to get FGC players to help them win the team prize by having representation across all EWC games. Street Fighter 6 remains a critical title in the lineup. Securing a skilled free agent before the April 30 roster lock guarantees GodLike an opportunity to earn points in the fighting game bracket. 

GodLike punctuated their announcement with campaign hashtags including #DilSeEWC and #DilSeRedBull. The branding makes it clear they are completely invested in this multi-game strategy.

---

## Adding Proven Guile Expertise

The acquisition provides the team with immediate tournament stakes. Caba has maintained a consistent presence in the global top 25 over the last few months. His defensive Guile playstyle makes him a bracket threat against the best players in the world. 

He recently delivered a signature performance at Frosty Faustings XVIII. Caba navigated a bracket of over 650 entrants to secure a top-three finish at the event. Partnering with a product of the dominant Dominican Republic training ecosystem gives GodLike a highly capable representative in Riyadh.
